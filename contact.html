Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order? +

Once your order has been shipped, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number and a link to track your package. You can also track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "Order History" section.

If you're having trouble tracking your order, please contact our customer support team with your order number.

What is your return policy? +

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused, in the same condition that you received it, and in its original packaging. Some items may have different return policies (like perishable goods or personalized items).

To initiate a return, please visit our Returns Center in your account dashboard or contact our customer support team.

How do I change or cancel my order? +

If your order hasn't been processed for shipping yet, you may be able to change or cancel it. Please contact our customer support team immediately with your order number and the changes you'd like to make.

Once an order has been shipped, it cannot be canceled, but you can return it after delivery following our return policy.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Rupay), net banking, UPI payments, and popular digital wallets like Paytm, PhonePe, and Google Pay. We also offer Cash on Delivery (COD) for eligible orders.

All payments are processed through secure payment gateways that comply with PCI-DSS standards.

How can I contact customer support? +

You can reach our customer support team through multiple channels:

  • Email: support@cartleap.com - We typically respond within 24 hours
  • Live Chat: Available on our website during business hours
  • Social Media: DM us on Twitter or Facebook
